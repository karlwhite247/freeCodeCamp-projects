About Us

Cloud Airlines is the flag carrier airline of Norway, headquartered in Oslo, Norway, near its main hub at Oslo Gardermoen Airport. It is the 2nd largest airline in Norway, based on fleet size and passengers carried. In January 2000 SA merged with Cambrian Airlines, creating the world's 7th-largest airline group in terms of annual revenue and the 6th-largest in Europe. It is listed on the Oslo Stock Exchange (OSE) and in the FTSE 100 Index.
